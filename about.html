About the Studio

Fewer things,
made better.

Practice

cashtoncatt is a Los Angeles jewelry studio making pieces in small, numbered editions. We produce a handful of designs each year — considered forms that hold their shape and value across decades of wear.

Each piece begins with a decision about form. We ask: what is the minimum required to make this object meaningful? The answer is usually less than expected. A single arc of silver wire. A circle, interrupted. Two rings, overlapping.

We do not have seasonal collections in the conventional sense. We make things when we are ready to make them well, and we stop when the edition is complete.

Materials

We work exclusively with sterling silver (925) and 18ct gold vermeil — a thick layer of 18 carat gold over a sterling silver base. Both materials are certified and sourced from suppliers who adhere to responsible standards.

Sterling silver will develop a patina over time. We consider this a feature rather than a defect. The material records the life of the wearer. If you prefer a bright finish, all pieces can be polished at home with a soft cloth or returned to us for professional restoration at no charge.

Gold vermeil is not gold-filled or gold-plated with thin deposits. Our vermeil is 3 micron minimum — substantially thicker than industry standard. It is designed to last.

Sterling Silver

92.5% pure silver. Hallmarked. Develops natural patina. Polishable.

Gold Vermeil

3μm 18ct gold over sterling. Thick-plated. Responsibly sourced.

Process

All pieces are made to order. When you place an order, we confirm availability within 24 hours and begin production. Your piece is then made, finished by hand, and numbered within the edition.

Lead time is typically 3–4 weeks from confirmed order. Each piece ships in a minimal linen pouch with a numbered certificate of edition. We do not use plastic packaging.

We offer one complimentary resize or adjustment within 90 days of receipt. After that period, restoration and adjustment services are available at cost.

Orders

You can purchase directly through this site. Each piece is handmade to order — add your piece to the cart, submit your order details, and we'll confirm availability and send a payment link within 48 hours.

For custom sizing, special requests, or press enquiries, write directly to hello@chastoncatt.com or use the contact page.
